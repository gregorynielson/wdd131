Detroit Cars and Cocoa

Site Name

This site name is for information regarding local car meets in the Detroit area. It is a play on the popular nickname for car meets 'Cars and Coffee'.

Site Purpose

This website provides information about local car meets and the various activities that will go along with them. Includes registration for events.

Scenarios

When and where is the next car meet?

How can I register to bring my own car?
